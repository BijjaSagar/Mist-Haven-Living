Global upload rules

  • Images: max 5 MB — PNG, JPEG, WebP, SVG, ICO · POST /api/admin/upload (admin only)
  • PDFs (catalogs/spec sheets): max 15 MB — not for photos
  • After upload: click Save on the form so the URL is stored
